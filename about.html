About WanderNest

WanderNest is your intelligent guide to finding perfect accommodations — built with passion, powered by AI. We help students, immigrants, and travelers locate living spaces that truly match their needs.

🌍 What We Do

Using machine learning and K-Means clustering, we analyze accommodation options and match them with your preferences — whether it's gym access, vegetarian food, university proximity, or budget constraints.

💡 Why WanderNest?

  • Smart recommendations based on real data
  • Interactive map to visualize clusters
  • AI chatbot for travel assistance
  • Easy filtering by location, amenities, and more

🚀 Our Mission

We aim to empower newcomers and explorers with tools that make relocation stress-free. We believe everyone deserves a cozy, convenient, and affordable nest — wherever they land.
